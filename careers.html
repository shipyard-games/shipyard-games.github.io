iOS Developer

Shipyard Games is a fresh new gaming studio backed by Supercell and we’re looking for an iOS Developer to join our team in Helsinki. As a developer in our small team, you would be contributing heavily to the final product and would have a unique possibility to leave your own mark in the gaming industry. Our vision is to build mobile games that people love to play for a long time.

The job requires:

  • iOS programming skills (references to apps published in the App Store)
  • Passion for games

It is also good to know:

  • SceneKit
  • Metal / OpenGL
  • Swift 3.x
  • 3D Graphics programming

Why join our team:

  • You’ll have complete professional freedom and responsibility
  • We think that amazing people are more attracted with a great company culture
  • Everyone contributes to the game design and has a possibility to influence the product
  • Benefits include health insurance and free dental plan. We want to take good care of our team!
